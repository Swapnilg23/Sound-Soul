Add an “AI Music Release Notes” feature to Sound2Soul.

Goal:
Give creators a structured place to document the creative process, AI involvement, human contribution, rights notes, and release preparation for every track.

Add fields to the track create/edit form:

Section title:
“AI Music Release Notes”

Fields:
1. AI tools used
Multi-select or free text:
Examples: Suno, Udio, ElevenLabs, Soundraw, AIVA, ChatGPT, DAW, GarageBand, Logic Pro, Ableton, Other

2. What did AI help create?
Checkboxes:
- Lyrics
- Melody
- Instrumental
- Vocals
- Arrangement
- Mixing/mastering
- Cover art
- Ideas/prompts only
- Other

3. What did the human creator contribute?
Checkboxes:
- Wrote or edited lyrics
- Selected prompts and creative direction
- Chose final outputs
- Edited arrangement
- Added vocals
- Added instruments
- Mixed/mastered audio
- Curated emotional story
- Created cover art
- Other

4. Source material notes
Textarea placeholder:
“Mention any samples, references, uploaded audio, stems, loops, or outside material used. Do not include anything you do not have rights to use.”

5. Vocal identity notes
Textarea placeholder:
“Describe whether vocals are human, synthetic, AI-generated, transformed, or based on your own voice. Do not upload tracks imitating real people without permission.”

6. Cover art source
Dropdown:
- Created by me
- AI-assisted
- Stock/licensed
- Commissioned
- Other

7. Final audio version
Text input:
Example: final_master_v1.wav

8. Distribution status
Dropdown:
- Not distributed yet
- Preparing for distribution
- Distributed
- Private/demo only

9. Release plan notes
Textarea:
“Optional notes about launch date, audience, mood, campaign, or distribution plans.”

Store this data in the database as release_notes jsonb or separate columns.

Show a summarized version in the public Track Trust Card:
- AI tools disclosed
- AI role disclosed
- Human contribution listed
- Vocal/source notes available
- Distribution status

Important:
Public display should summarize safely.
Do not reveal private-sensitive notes unless the creator chooses public visibility.
Add a toggle:
“Show release notes summary publicly”
Default: ON for summary, OFF for detailed notes.
